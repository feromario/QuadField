# Setup
*  Autonomous 1v1 PvP  
*  Each player has 4 troops: Tank, Warrior, Mage, King.  
*  Players take turns drawing cards from a deck of 108 cards.  
*  Who goes first is decided by format or coin flip.
---
# General rules
*  Certain cards correlate to certain troops.
*  If a Tank card gets drawn but there is no Tank troop on the field, the player's turn gets skipped.
*  There are special cards that don't correlate to any troop.
*  Troops are ordered in a line, which means the first in line gets damaged first. (excluding specific cases)
*  Troops can be healed over their original health with the max being 5 HP.
*  No carry over damage into the next troop.   
*  If the King dies, the game is over.
---
# Troop stats

Tank (T) // 5 HP  
Warrior (W) // 4 HP  
Mage (M) // 3HP  
King (K) // 5 HP

# Card Stats
_(number of cards in a deck and their abilities subject to change)_

**Special cards**  
- Revive X (24 cards)  
4 cards for each troop, excluding King, need 3 of the same troop to revive that troop.  
Can be saved for later use if the troop is still alive.

- Joker (4 cards)  
Doubles the effect of the next card, player draws again  
Not applicable to another joker or revive card


**Tank cards**  
- Dash (8 cards)  
Deal 1 dmg.
- Heal (8 cards)  
Heal itself 1 hp.
- Necromancer (4 cards)  
Spawn a 1 hp foot solider who gets first in line.
- Life Steal (2 card)  
Steal 1 hp from enemy troop first in line.


**Warrior cards**
- Slash (8 cards)  
Deal 2 dmg.
- Swing (8 cards)  
Deal 1 dmg each to the first two in line.
- Inspire (4 cards)  
Draw 2 cards.
- Cleave (2 card)  
Deal 4 dmg.


**Mage cards**
- Support (8 cards)  
Heal 1 hp to the first friendly troop in line (excluding itself).
- Fireball (8 cards)  
Deal 1 dmg to 2nd in line (if only King left, damage King instead).
- Magic Blast (4 cards)  
Deal 3 dmg.
- Thunder (2 card)  
Deal 1 dmg each to every enemy troop.


**King cards**
- Jab (8 cards)  
Deal 1 dmg.
- Hook (4 cards)  
Deal 2 dmg.
- Straight (2 card)  
Deal 3 dmg.
